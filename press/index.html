Company Overview

Boilerplate (Short)

Bodaty is a technology company that builds software products and delivers specialized technical services. Its flagship products — AICtrlNet (open source) and HitLai (commercial) — are a governed AI orchestration platform where AI agents, humans, and enterprise systems work together under unified governance. Bodaty also builds consumer applications including ChatMemoir, Samyata, and deyaPay, and delivers specialized technical services under federal government contracts.
