About Us

Bodaty LLC builds software that bridges AI capabilities with human expertise. We believe the future of AI is collaborative—not autonomous.

Founded by Bobby Koritala, we observed that AI systems consistently fail in production when they ignore human judgment. The problem isn't the AI—it's the architecture.

Our products—AICtrlNet and HitLai—power AI workflows where humans are first-class participants, not afterthoughts.
