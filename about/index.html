About Us

Bodaty LLC is a technology company that builds software products and delivers specialized technical services. Our portfolio spans enterprise AI, consumer applications, payments, and government services.

Bodaty (बोधति) — from Sanskrit: "to perceive, learn, awaken."

Our current flagship products are AICtrlNet, a governed AI orchestration platform, and HitLai, its enterprise-ready workflow interface. Together, they orchestrate AI, humans, and 10,000+ tools. The AI Dial lets every team set their own autonomy level — at whatever pace they're comfortable with.
