About Bodaty.

Bodaty LLC is a technology company that builds software products and delivers specialized technical services. Our portfolio spans enterprise AI, consumer applications, payments, and government services.

Bodaty (बोधति) — from Sanskrit: “to perceive, learn, awaken.”

Our flagship products are AICtrlNet, a governed AI orchestration platform, and HitLai, its SMB-and-mid-market product. Together, they coordinate AI agents, your team, and the systems each business already runs — at the pace each part of the business is comfortable with.
